# dS/CFT and the cosmological bootstrap

**Autori:** Daniel Anninos, Dionysios Anninos, Damian A. Galante

**Pubblicato:** 2024-01-15

**Categorie arXiv:** hep-th, gr-qc

**Link:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.08441](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.08441)

## Abstract

We study the dS/CFT correspondence from the perspective of the cosmological bootstrap. The wavefunction of the universe at future infinity is shown to satisfy bootstrap equations analogous to those of a Euclidean CFT. We analyze the constraint equations arising from de Sitter isometries and derive the analytic structure of cosmological correlators. Our results suggest a precise version of the dS/CFT conjecture in which the Hartle-Hawking wavefunction is dual to a Euclidean CFT deformed by relevant operators. We compute three-point functions of scalar operators and compare with bulk perturbation theory.
