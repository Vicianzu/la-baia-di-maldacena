# BMS symmetry and soft theorems in the gravitational S-matrix

**Autori:** Andrew Strominger

**Pubblicato:** 2024-01-14

**Categorie arXiv:** hep-th, gr-qc

**Link:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.07962](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.07962)

## Abstract

We establish that Weinberg's soft graviton theorem is the Ward identity of the BMS supertranslation symmetry. This is one corner of the infrared triangle: the three-way equivalence among (i) soft theorems for gravitons in the S-matrix, (ii) Ward identities for large diffeomorphisms at null infinity, and (iii) the gravitational memory effect. We extend the analysis to sub-leading soft gravitons and show they are related to superrotations, elements of the extended BMS group. Applications to black hole physics and the information paradox are discussed.
