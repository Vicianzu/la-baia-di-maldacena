# Flat space holography and the BMS group at spatial infinity

**Autori:** Marc Geiller, Céline Zwikel

**Pubblicato:** 2024-01-14

**Categorie arXiv:** hep-th, gr-qc

**Link:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.06543](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.06543)

## Abstract

We study the asymptotic symmetry group of flat spacetime at spatial infinity. Building on the work of Ashtekar and Romano, we show that the BMS group at spatial infinity is isomorphic to the BMS group at null infinity, establishing a link between the two descriptions of flat space asymptotics. We compute the charges and fluxes associated to BMS symmetries at spatial infinity and show that the symplectic structure is well defined. These results provide new evidence for a holographic description of flat space gravity in terms of a theory living on the two-sphere at spatial infinity.
